How to use Nana's Ninja

1. Ask for a Recipe

Use the typing box at the bottom of the Home screen to tell me what you want to cook. For example, type "How do I cook a chicken breast?".

2. Use What You Have

Not sure what to make? Just list the ingredients you have in the fridge or cupboard, like "I have chicken, potatoes, and carrots." and I'll suggest a meal.

3. The Magic Camera

Tap the 📸 button to take a photo of some food. I'll look at it and tell you exactly what settings to use on your Ninja to cook it perfectly.

4. Follow-up Questions

After I give you a recipe, you can use the same typing box to ask more questions about it, like "Can I cook this from frozen?" or "What if I want it extra crispy?".

5. The Favourites Tin

If you like a recipe, tap the ⭐ button to save it. You can find all your saved recipes later by tapping 'Tin' at the bottom of the screen.
